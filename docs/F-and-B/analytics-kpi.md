# Analytics & KPI Framework

> **Source of Truth cho:** Metrics, targets, formulas  
> **Ai cập nhật:** Ops Coordinator (theo đề xuất Analytics Agent)

---

## KPI Dashboard

| KPI | Định nghĩa | Formula | Target | Frequency đo | Tool đo |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| CPL | Chi phí/lead | Total spend / Total leads | < [X] | Weekly | GA/Ads |
| ROAS | Return on ad spend | Revenue / Ad spend | > 3:1 | Weekly | Ads |
| CTR | Click-through rate | Clicks / Impressions | > [X]% | Daily | Ads |
| CVR | Conversion rate | Conversions / Clicks | > [X]% | Weekly | GA |
| CAC | Customer acquisition cost | Total MKT cost / New customers | < [X] | Monthly | Internal |
| MQL Rate | Marketing qualified lead rate | MQLs / Total leads | > [X]% | Monthly | CRM |

## Reporting Schedule
- **Daily:** CTR, CPC, Spend
- **Weekly:** CPL, ROAS, CVR, Lead count
- **Monthly:** Full report (tất cả KPI + insights + recommendations)
- **Quarterly:** Strategic review + kế hoạch quý tiếp
